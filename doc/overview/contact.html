Contact Options

IRC

You can find us on IRC in #paludis on irc.freenode.net.

Mailing Lists

We have two mailing lists:

  • paludis-user, for general user questions.
  • paludis-sekrit, for development. Rather high noise, since it gets an auto-CC: on trac tickets and nightly build reports. Approval is required to subscribe.

Bug Reports

We use Trac for bug reports, feature requests and so on.
